What are we building?
We are building a Smart Customer Support Ticket Router.
Imagine you're the owner of a company like Amazon, Flipkart, or Swiggy.
Every day, thousands of customers submit complaints.

Examples:
"My payment was deducted twice."
"I cannot login to my account."
"The app crashes every time I open it."
"I want a refund."

Currently, a support executive reads each ticket manually.
Our system will automate this process.
Our Goal
The user will write a support ticket.

For example:
My payment was deducted twice and my order was not placed.

Our system will analyze it and return something like:
Category:
Payment Problem

Priority:
High

Assigned Team:
Finance Team

Suggested Resolution:
Duplicate charge reversed and refund initiated.

What is the AI actually doing?
The AI is learning from past customer tickets.

Suppose our dataset contains:
Issue Description	        Category
Cannot login to account	    Login Issue
Payment deducted twice	    Payment Problem
App crashes after update	Bug Report
Need my refund	            Refund Request

The model studies thousands of such examples.
After training, if someone enters a new ticket, the model predicts its category.

Example:
Input:
My transaction failed but money got deducted.

Output:
Payment Problem
